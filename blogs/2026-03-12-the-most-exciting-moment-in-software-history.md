Something shifted over Thanksgiving weekend. On November 24, 2025, Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.5 alongside major updates to Claude Code; for those of us who've spent decades building software, the ground moved. Not in a hype-cycle, keynote-presentation kind of way. In a sit-down-and-build-something-you-couldn't-build-last-week kind of way.
